About us

We are three students studying Information Technology in Zadar and this webpage is our project.

It's conceived as a traveling guide in which people can see a few details about beauties and things to do in Zadar county (and even a bit wider). With some photos and short descriptions, we thought this webpage would come in handy to tourists or even local people to see what they could do in their free time and whether these locations suit them.

We hope you will enjoy your time here and hopefully find some cool new places to visit or things to do in your free time!
