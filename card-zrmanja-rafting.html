Description

Start your Kayaking Zrmanja River trip in the village of Kastel Zegarski. Where you will find intact nature, clear waters and a bounty of flora and fauna. Therefore it makes River Zrmanja one of the most beautiful Croatian and European rivers. So, kayaking Zrmanja River, down its rapids and falls will be both a fun and memorable experience. The kayaking Zrmanja River trip is so safe even children as old as 6 can participate. But, even if you are experienced kayaker, you will enjoy the unique scenery of this remote area. Also, towards the end of the trip there is a 3 m (10 ft) cliff, perfect for jumping into the river.
