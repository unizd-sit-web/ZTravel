Description

Located in vicinity of Golubic and Kastel Zegarski settlements, Kudin most has been constructed between 18th and 19th centuries. It is more than 100 meters long, connecting the two banks of Krupa. The educational walk starts with a breathtaking view on the river and brings you to Kudin Most after a 40 minutes’ walk. Due to its architectural beauty and historical value, the bridge has been put under state protection.

Literally translated, Kudin Most means ‘Bridge of Kuda’. According to a legend, that was a name of young man who built the bridge in order to reach a girl he loved very much. It is reminder that no river is too broad for true love.
