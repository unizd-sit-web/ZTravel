Description

Rafting on Cetina River is the most popular and most attended adventure tour in Split area. Rafting in protected area of the Cetina river canyon attracts many tourists every summer. The quiet parts of the river and the second and third grade rapids enable a three-hour combination of adventure in the amazing Cetina river canyon. Rafting tour on Cetina river is filled with peaceful green pools perfect for swimming, splashing or floating alongside in perfect tranquility between rapids.
Rafting on Cetina river is suitable for beginners and can be also enjoyed by experienced adventurers.
